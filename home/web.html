Welcome!

The COSMOS CLUB is part of the MIT-WPU Pune. Here, we attempt to bring about an interest and awareness in the field of Astronomy and Space Research.

All along the we have been promoting amateur astronomy through talks, competetions and lectures. Astronomy is fun, but it can be even more fun when you do it with other people. So here we are for you to be amazed along with you.
